What startup is he working on full-time?!

My current project taking up 75% of my time is Percuri, an automated intern management tool. I noticed that lots of internship experiences can be disorganized and messy, leading to unsatisfying outcomes. As an intern, you want to get a return offer and as a manager, you want to have the latest up-to-date info on your best interns. Essentially, just "winging it" is not the ideal way to go through an internship, so we give interns a place for all their information from training and learning to be in the same place. Our AI assistant is perfect for helping a new intern adjust to a company and succeed. You can click on the button to take you to the landing page to get more information. Even if you're not looking for internships, I would love any feedback you have on the concept.
